## Schemas
LT schemas contains Look up (definition) Tables like city, blog type. 
These tables are used in the screen most of the time as a drop down list.

Our design will have following schemas:

- LT_CONTENT_MANAGENT
- CONTENT_MANAGENT

- LT_COMMON
- COMMON

Every module schema will have corresponding LT schema.
